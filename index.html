Welcome Journalers

Welcome to The Interactive Journal, a place to record all aspects of your life! There are so many different ways to approach a blank journal. Will your journal be a place to record daily events? Dreams? Emotions? Goals? Will your journal be more artistic or wordy? A planner? All of these journaling options can be intimidating. But look no further! The Interactive Journal guides you through different prompts and activities in order for you to gain the most out of your journaling experience! The Interactive Journal also gives you suggestions for how to get involved in the real world with real people.

So what are you waiting for? Get started on your Interactive Journal today! All you need to do is click on each section in the navigation bar for different journaling activities and ideas. Happy journaling!
